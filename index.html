My Passion in IT

Reason

As a kid, I had always been curious about how different things work, how the human body works, how old is the universe. We are living in an era where we heavily relied on technology to satisfy and helps us in our daily life. However, technology and how they work have never been something that interests me as a child, partly might be because it was too complicated for the brain of a young adolescent like me to understand.

When I start the IGCSE ICT class in grade 9, it never hit me off as something appealing. It is not until Iâ€™m in grade 10 when I started to learn about basic HTML coding and turtle graphics, that it becomes clear to me how mesmerizing the world of technology can be. I was fascinated by how just a few lines of code can create something so intricate and exquisite, and I become absorbed in learning more about this subject. The A's I received in the IGCSE test in ICT and Mathematics also a huge motivation for me to choose IT as my major in University.

Another driving force for me to follow this path is through various YouTube videos and channels about IT and engineering topics I watch over the years. The channel Code Bullet is my biggest inspiration that led me to become attracted to the concept of artificial intelligence. And the further I research about AI, the more mesmerized I became with this concept and their accomplishments in improving human life.

My Dream Job

Google has been the dream of many students studying in the IT major and relative majors, and I'm not an exception. Because of my interest in AI and machine learning, I want to become a software engineering that specializes in these fields, especially the development of new AI.

I have always been fascinated by how just from lines of code can create AIs, a program that is so intelligent that they can help us solve problems that might take a long time to crack. Therefore, I want to be a part of the artificial intelligence development team and be able to contribute to the process.

Base on Google's description, I would need to have an MS or Ph.D. degree in computer science or a related field. I also need to have at least 2 years of working or educational experience with AI and at least 1 year of work in a relevant field, for example as a software developer. Knowing numerous programming languages and having experience with many technical skills are preferable in the field.

I am still new to programming and only have some experience with HTML and Python. Therefore, I'm planning to self-taught myself more programming languages in addition to the languages I'll learn in university. I'll will strive to advance the skills and knowledge I already have with both HTML and Python. Moreover, I want to have some basic experience with coding an AI. Therefore, I want to make some small project for me to get to know more about AI and their process. And because the job require at least 1 year of working experience, I want to begin with applying and working as a software engineering first to gain more experience, knowledge, and insight. Hence I intend to apply for an internship in some good company during my transfer time in Australia through the Flagship internship course RMIT offer in year 3. I hope that through the internship I can improve my hard skills and soft skills, and acquire seniors' advices in the workplace.

Future Project Idea

Overview:

My idea for the project is to create a RPG game using Unity. In this game, the main character is an orphan boy who live in the world full of Dlaejm. He live in an orphan home in a small village deep in the forest. One day, while played in the forest, the village was attacked by these creature and his family got killed by them.
